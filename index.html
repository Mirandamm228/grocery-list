Your Grocery List!



Welcome to Your Grocery List! If you want to add an item, just type it in and click the button 'Add to list!'. Add as much as you like. If you made a mistake, you can remove the item from the list by clicking the 'x' button that is next to the item. If you want to reset the whole list, just click the 'Clear List' button. Being able to cross off items on your list will be available soon. Enjoy!
